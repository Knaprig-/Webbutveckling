Gustav Grännsjö

Hello there! My name is Gustav and I am the creator of this site. At the time of writing this I am 18 years old and study at NTI-gymnasiet Kronhusgatan in Gothenburg, Sweden.

As you might be able to tell from the main menu, I am a big fan of the card game Magic: the Gathering and I figured that it would be fun to design this website after those cards. Apart from card games I like playing computer games, reading and cooking.

This site contains information about things that I've done, a blog and how to contact me. So feel free to explore the site and enjoy yourself!
